CACHE-HINT — three-zone variable classification:

You can mentally label your intermediate variables with one of three
tags:

- PROBATION — variables you just created, whose usefulness is not
  yet proven.
- MAIN — variables you have already referenced at least once after
  creation.
- GHOST — short memo of a variable you once had but no longer do.

The tag is just a label, not a command. Use it if the classification
is helpful for thinking about which variables to reuse when
structuring your next query.
